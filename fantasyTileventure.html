Escape from the Ancient Dungeon of Mystery

After a long dungeon expedition, all your equipment was destroyed by a random acid trap! Escape back to the surface and live to fight another day!

Move with the arrow keys and wait a turn with the spacebar. If the monster catches you it will hurt you, and a new monster will appear. Reach the stairs up before you run out of life to escape!
